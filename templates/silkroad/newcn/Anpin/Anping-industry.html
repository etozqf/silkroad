Industry Overview

Anping’s wire mesh industry can trace its history back to the Ming Dynasty, or over 500 years ago. Over the years, Anping has developed a wide range of wire mesh products that fall into six series comprising over 500 varieties and 6,000 specifications. They are widely used in a raft of sectors such as modern agriculture, environmental protection. petrochemical, building materals, auto parts, medical and health, aerospace, national defense, etc. At present, Anping’s woven wire mesh products account for more than 80 percent of China’s total production and exports.

Anping is known as the “Capital of China Wire Mesh,” “Famous China Wire Mesh Woven City,” “China Wire Mesh Product Base,” “China Wire Mesh Export Base” and “The National Foreign Trade Transformation and Upgrade Professional Example Base.”

As the mainstream and pillar industry of Anping, the wire mesh industry consumes more than 800 kt of plates, over 4.5 mt of wire products and some 1.1 billion square meters of knitmesh annually. Currently, Anping has over 13,000 enterprises and more than 210,000 people involved in producing and dealing in wire mesh, with the number of manufacturers topping 100 and that of sales outlets at home and abroad exceeding 15,000. More than 1,100 companies are engaged in wire mesh exporting business in the county, which sell products to 210 countries and regions worldwide and generate over 1 billion U.S. dollars in export delivery value annually. For the moment, the annual output value by the local wire mesh industry tops 50 billion yuan.

In order to quicken the pace of upgrading and transforming the wire mesh industry, guide enterprises to increase investments and industrial innovation, and enhance the industrial scale and intelligent level, the government of Anping County has ibuilt and improved ten platforms as follows:

Policy Platform

The county has set up enterprise financing risk fund to offer financing guarantee for bank loans to companies introducing advanced equipment as well as competitive enterprises.

Industrial Park Platform

The county has started construction of the Anping Economic Development Zone, which covers a planned area of 23.51 square km. So far, more than 500 million yuan has ben invested for infrastructure construction, 63 projects have settled in the zone with total investments of 40 billion yuan, and 58 enterprises have started operation in the zone.

Logistics Platform

An international logistics industrial zone covering an area of 3,000 mu with total investments of 5.2 billion yuan has been built, which has opened more than 500 transportation lines with annual cargo handling capacity exceeding 16 million tonnes. An inland port with investment of 1 billion yuan is under way.

R&D Platform

The county’s R&D instituions have signed contracts with eight domestic institutions of higher learning. Since 2015, more than 60 new products and new technologies have been developed with 97 authorized patents.

Testing Platform

The county has invested more than 16 million yuan to build a provincial-level mesh product quality testing center. Meanwhile, construction of a state-level mesh laboratory is under way.

Market Platform

Anping's wire mesh market saw transactions reach 7 billion yuan in 2015, making it the largest mesh distributing center in the world.

At present, an international mesh mall is under construction, which requires a total investment of 1.6 billion yuan and covers an area of 437,000 square meters.

Exhibition Platform

The China Anping International Wire Mesh Fair has been held for 15 years since 2001. The China Anping International Wire Mesh Fair 2015 saw transactions stand at 1.18 billion yuan, of which foreign trade transactions amounted to 100 million U.S. dollars.

Information Platform

The county has forged three network information platforms, including sousiwang.com, cn-wiremesh.com and wd56.net. Hebei Mashi E-commerce Limited Liability Company is one of the country’s 83 e-commerce demonstration enterprises as designated by the Ministry of Commerce. Trade contracts signed via network accounts for more than 40 percent of all sales.

Talents Platform

A vocational education center with total investments of 160 million yuan and covering an area of 180 mu has been built and offers courses about numerical control, electric welding, knitting technology, and e-commerce. The center offers more than 3,000 medium and high practical technical personnel annually to the wire mesh industry.

Financing Platform

The county encourages local financial institutions to expand input in the wire mesh industry while taking measures to attract financial institutions outside of the county to set up outlets in Anping. By April 2016, the financial institutions in Anping had seen their outstanding amount of corporate loans stand at 11.93 billion yuan with the loan-to-deposit ratio at 62.21 percent. In addition, the county has built d a financial supermarket where 11 financial institutions have settled in.
