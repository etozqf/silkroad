Silkroad Weekly is one of the "Xinhua Silk Road" information service products launched by China Economic Information Service. Capitalizing on Xinhua News Agency’s worldwide news and information gathering networks as well as field research advantage, the periodical aims to provide authoritative, professional and exclusive information about China's Belt and Road Initiative.

Comprising seven columns, the periodical covers a range of specifically selected topics from government policies and company activities to projects and investment concerning the Belt and Road Initiative, in addition to experts’ opinions on Belt and Road-related projects or policies and involves international cooperation in production capacity.
