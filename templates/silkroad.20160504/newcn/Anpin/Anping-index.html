Industry Overview

Anping County has a history of more than 2,200 years since the West Han Dynasty. Over 500 years ago, wire mesh industry began to emerge in Anping. Now, Anping is known as the “Capital of China Wire Mesh”, “Famous China Wire Mesh Woven City”, “China Wire Mesh Product Base”, “China Wire Mesh Export Base” and “The National Foreign Trade Transformation and Upgrade Professional Example Base”...
