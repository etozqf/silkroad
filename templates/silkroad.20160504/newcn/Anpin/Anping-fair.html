About Fair

China Anping International Wire Mesh Fair is the only professional international exhibition of wire mesh products in the world. Sponsored by the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, Hebei Provincial People's Government, China Light Industry Association, China Chamber of Commerce of Metals Minerals & Chemicals Importers & Exporters (CCCMC) and China National Hardware Association, the annual event has lived up to its reputation as the barometer of the world wire mesh industry for rich exhibits and numerous merchants.

The first International Anping Wire Mesh Fair was successfully held at Wire Mesh World (an exhibition center) in October 2001. The fair received business visitors from home and abroad, including 168 foreign business people from the U.S., Canada, Sweden, Ukraine, Australia, India, Japan, South Korea, Mongolia, and Vietnam. Some business people from Hong Kong and Taiwan were also present at the first fair.

The 16th China Anping International Wire Mesh Fair will be held in Anping Exhibition Center from October 26 to 28, 2016. This year’s Fair is designed to promote new wire mesh products, technologies, equipment and manufacturing process, further boost international industry exchanges and cooperation, enhance Anping’s position in the global wire mesh market and propel transformation and upgrading of the local wire mesh industry.

Famous wire mesh manufacturers at home and abroad will be invited to attend the Fair.

The indoor exhibition area will be divided into four parts including the craftsman high-quality products and "Four New" product exhibition area, filter material and filter exhibition area, wire mesh deep-processing finished product exhibition area, and traditional wire mesh product exhibition area.

Meanwhile, a fence netting anti-aging experimental area will be built outside the exhibition center and be put into service before commencement of the Fair.
